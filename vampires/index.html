Famous vampires


Graf Dracula

He is the most famous vampire of them all. His fullname is Vlad Dracula and he lives in Transilvania.


Die Vampirschwestern

English: the vampire sisters, is a german family movie. The family including 3 vampires (the father and his two daughters) and their human mother are moving to an other city where they have to learn how to life with normal people.


Edward Cullen

He is the maincharacter of the Twilight Saga, romance fantasy films based on four novels. He is fighting over the love of one of the other maincharacters called Bella.
